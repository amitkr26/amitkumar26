# Amit Kumar | Semiconductor & Electronics Engineer

Engineering research platform documenting work in semiconductor thin-film fabrication, embedded systems, and RTL design.

## Research Focus

FeCo/CoFe magnetic thin film fabrication on flexible substrates via DC Magnetron Sputtering. Characterization: XRD, VSM, FMR, AFM, FESEM/EDS.

## Technical Domains

- **Semiconductor Fabrication** — DC sputtering, thin film deposition, vacuum systems, flexible electronics
- **Embedded Systems** — ESP32, Arduino, 8051, AVR — sensor integration, PID control, wireless, PCB design
- **Digital VLSI** — Verilog HDL, RTL design, FSM, timing analysis, FPGA (Artix-7)
- **Simulation & Analysis** — MATLAB, PSpice, Xilinx Vivado, OriginPro

## Architecture

- **Framework:** Next.js 14 (App Router)
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS, graphite-slate-cyan palette
- **Dependencies:** Framer Motion, react-hook-form, sonner
